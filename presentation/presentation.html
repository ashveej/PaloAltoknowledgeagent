An AI assistant for our sales teams that answers product questions only from approved company sources — and always shows its work and how sure it is.

Everything in this deck is backed by the working system and real data.

The problem

Sales teams get hard questions — live, on the spot

⏱️

Questions come fast

"Does our product support X in version Y?" — asked in the middle of a customer call.

🗂️

Answers are scattered & go stale

Spread across manuals, release notes, and wikis — across products and versions. It ages.

📉

A wrong answer costs the deal

Confidently saying the wrong thing loses technical credibility — and trust is hard to win back.

A generic chatbot makes this worse — it sounds confident even when it's guessing, with no proof.

The idea

An expert assistant that never guesses

Think of it as a diligent research assistant who:

  • 📗 only quotes approved company sources
  • 🧾 shows the exact quote behind every answer
  • 🎯 tells you how confident it is — and why
  • 🙋 asks a human expert when it isn't sure
🛡️

"No proof,
no answer."

The one rule the whole system is built around.

Every answer comes with the receipt

The exact source text is shown inline — a salesperson can verify the answer without leaving the screen.

It tells you how sure it is

Three confidence levels — calculated, not guessed

● High

Current, official sources fully back the answer. Use it with confidence.

● Medium

Backed, but the source is older or less authoritative. Double-check the detail.

● Low

Not enough proof. Sent to a human expert instead of answered.

📅
Real example: a decryption answer was correct — but its source hadn't been reviewed in 241 days, so the system automatically downgraded it from High to ● Medium. Freshness is built into the score.
